About us

"Hunger is caused by poverty and inequality, not scarcity"


Hunger in America is a problem, so we here at Food For All aim to put a dent into the problem and try to solve it programatically. Our solution is this application. It is meant to portray the amount of food by weight that a local food shelter has.


Just enter in your city and you will be shown the amount of food that a food shelter has on a map. The size of the sandwich indicates about how much food the shelter has. The bigger the sandwich, the more food the shelter has. Click on the sandwich for an exact number of food by weight the shelter has
